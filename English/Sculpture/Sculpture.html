Cretan Chairs

Spring 2007

These chairs are the result of a creative cooperation with Martin WEBSTER, who lives in a small village of Crete.

Martin rescues broken-up chairs, rebuilds them, paints them, redoes their seat, with a lot of skill and a great sense of colour and aesthetics. I really enjoyed adding a final touch to them by painting a simple floral motif on the back of them.

This is the first time I manage to work so closely and productively with someone and sincerely hope we can take this a little further...
